Goal of application

You can very quickly check opinions about the product you are interested in, all you need is ID of product from ceneo.pl and this application will show you information you need.

By product list module you can easily filter and order your products by properties that are important for you!

You can mark product as liked one, so it helps you to choose the best one!

Functionalities

Extracting opinions

You can extract opinions from ceneo.pl on any product by providing its ID from URL

Try it!
Saved products

You can see list of saved products with attached opinions
